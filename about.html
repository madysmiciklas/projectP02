About Nectar

Nectar started in 2008, when Jane Smith, CEO of Nectar, noticed the lack of sustainiblity and eco-friendly art supplies offered on the market. Not only that, but she found it hard to track down the products that were sustainable, especially at large-name corporations. This was the beginning of the Nectar, the largest online art supplies retailer of all things sustainable, eco-friendly and natural!

Our Promises:
